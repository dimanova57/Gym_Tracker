Bicep curls

Starting Position:

Stand upright with a dumbbell in each hand, arms fully extended, and your palms facing forward.

Execution:

Keep your upper arms close to your body and your elbows stationary. Bend your arms at the elbows, bringing the dumbbells towards your shoulders. Contract your biceps as you lift the weights. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position with controlled movement. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Tips:

Maintain proper posture with your back straight and core engaged. Avoid swinging your upper body or using momentum to lift the weights. Breathe naturally; exhale as you lift the dumbbells and inhale as you lower them. Start with a weight that is manageable, and gradually increase it as you gain strength.
